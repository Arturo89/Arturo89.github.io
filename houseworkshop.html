Barrio La California

Project

La California in San Jose, Costa Rica is a meeting point for many young people of the city. Different social groups share the space between the local bars of the area on the night time. This has turned La Cali into an open, tolerant and diverse space.

The project is intended for local artists seeking both housing and a workshop space for their own projects. The workshop becomes the heart of the house and everything revolves around this space, this center can be seen from any room of the house. The space becomes flexible to be transformed as an exhibition space at the same time.
