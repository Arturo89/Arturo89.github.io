Costa Rican Red Cross

The Costa Rican Red Cross is a private humanitarian organization, it is auxiliary to the public authorities. It is affiliated to a global movement that is home to 185 nations and governed under the Fundamental Principles of Humanity, Impartiality, Neutrality, Independence, Voluntary Service, Unity and Universality.

Design for Emergencies

Module designed from a 40-foot wagon to house 24 rescuers in emergencies, fulfilling basic needs and providing a comfortable environment to rest.

The project  consist of a  truck wagon, easy to install and mobilize, which has space for 2 modules of 12 cabins each and a bathroom area with 3 showers, 3 toilets and 2 sinks.

The project is mobile , for the use in emergencies, where necessary.
