Interactive Surface

The design of an Interactive Surface that reacts to a human stimulus generating light and movement in space.

Performance

The ability of the fabric to respond to a human stimulus generates tension on the surface that regulate the opacity of the material. The passage of light is regulated by seams that generate a radial geometry of the original surface of the fabric.

Models

The material, while it is pulled by the seams, stretches and allows the entry of light. These seams are connected to a servo that by programming via Arduino responds to human approach. Distance is read by an ultrasonic sensor.

A virtual model was made with Grasshopper to explore the geometry of the seams as well as the reaction with the ultrasonic sensor.
