Energy Expansion

The canton of Tilaran has been the scene of exploitation of renewable energy for 30 years. The Instituto Costarricense de Electricidad (ICE) entered the canton with the creation of the artificial lake under the Arenal hydroelectric project. The project, divided into three hydroelectric plants of 363MW in total, represents 14% of the installed capacity in the country.

The area has an exceptional wind resource. In 1996 Tilarán wind farm was installed,which produces 20 MW of effective power that represents 1% of all the consumption in the country. In 1998 Aeroenergía adds to the production of wind energy with a park of 6MW. This was followed by Tierras Morenas in 1999 with a production of 20MW and Tejona in 2002 with a production of 20MW.

Map Diagrams

There is now a National Plan: Plan de expansión de la generación eléctrica. Periodo 2012-2024 which allows the entry of new renewable energy projects to the national grid.

Map Diagrams of the area where created to analyze renewable energy projects based on hydropower, wind, geothermal and biomass. As in the different stages that these projects are nowadays within the territory.

The topography of the area is analyzed to find the points of greatest wind potential that can predict the path of the energy expansion in the area.
